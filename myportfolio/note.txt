flex: 1 1 200px;

What Each Part Means:
flex-grow: 1
The item can grow to fill available space relative to other items.
1 means it will grow proportionally with others also set to grow.

flex-shrink: 1
The item can shrink if necessary (e.g., on smaller screens).
1 means it will shrink proportionally with others.

flex-basis: 200px
The initial/default size of the item before growing or shrinking.
Here, each item will try to be 200px wide.
